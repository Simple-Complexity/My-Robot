Welcome to My Robot

Have you ever remembered that you left your computer on as you were just about to walk outside? Well not anymore, My Robot is ready to control your devices on your command. You'll never need to get out of bed after watching a movie, ever! Simply say Alexa, tell My Robot to turn off my desktop and My Robot will do it for you.

What can My Robot do?

Currently My Robot can turn off, sleep, lock, and restart Mac and Windows computers. I am working to include functionality and am happily taking suggestions, just create a new Github issue

Get My Robot now

Download the My Robot client for Mac or Windows, log in with your amazon account, and get the Alexa skill. It's that easy.
